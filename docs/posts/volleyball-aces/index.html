The final product shows the three Maryland players that have played in at least 20 matches for the Terps and have an acespermatch of at least .9545. Each of the three Terps have an acespermatches of at least 1, but come in on the lower side of the chart. Camryn Hawthorne of Indiana has so far this season averaged an amazing 2.2 aces per match in 24 matches played.
